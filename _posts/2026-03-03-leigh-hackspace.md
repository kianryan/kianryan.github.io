As alluded to on the Now page, after my RTA in 2022, the sporting side of 
my life slowed down.  In the early part of my recovery, I walked in to 
[Leigh Hackspace](https://www.leighhack.org/), which was in it's early 
days of coming out of Covid lock down.

![Refurbishing a sleigh, in the dark.  Perfectly normal](/assets/images/2026/03/sleigh.jpg)

A [Hackspace](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Hackerspace) is peculiar place. 
Each one has it's own identity, but 
principally a Hackspace or Makerspace is a place where people come together 
and share making tools and skills.  


![CNC with Boop Sensor](/assets/images/2026/03/fabrication.jpg)

Ours has a nice balance of 
technology and arts, with [3d printers, laser, CNC as well as traditional 
woodworking, textiles and a darkroom](https://www.leighhack.org/facilities/). 
We host artists, including the local parade group. This dynamic makes for 
an eclectic group, and for some 
interesting builds, 
where you can be building stage props for a #1 UK band one week, and 
helping to build a bubble blowing caterpillar bike the next.

![By Chris Allen, CC BY-SA 2.0, https://commons.wikimedia.org/w/index.php?curid=13176326](/assets/images/2026/03/spinners.jpg)

Leigh Hackspace is located in [Spinners Mill](https://www.spinnersmill.co.uk/), 
a Grade 2 listed building outside the 
centre of Leigh.  Spinners is home to a collection of boutique businesses and 
artists spread over five floors - we've got our own cinema on the fourth floor. 

![Roof on pod, just having finished being built](/assets/images/2026/03/roof.jpg)

The mill, and the Hackspace, are constantly evolving over time.  We've just 
finished two large builds, rebuilding our social area and building new pods in 
our workshops space. The social area is now dual use, and can be used for 
large builds by clearing the central space, and the new pods in workshop area 
allow us to now set up a metalworking space, and add more private space.

![CNC with Boop Sensor](/assets/images/2026/03/boop.jpg)

If you're in the Leigh, Wigan, Bolton or Manchester areas, please feel free 
to [drop in and see us](https://www.leighhack.org/visit/).  We run tours on Saturday from 12PM til 1PM.  If 
you're looking for a space to run a local user group meeting, or have a 
project you would like to run, [please feel free to get in touch](mailto:info@leighhack.org).

It's also why things have been a little bit quiet here and on Twitch.  I'm 
at the space several times a week, and that takes a fair amount of energy. 
I do continue to post over on [Mastodon](https://oldbytes.space/@kianryan), and my goal to 2026 is to post a little 
more frequently here.
